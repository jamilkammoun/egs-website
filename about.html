Who We Are

A New Kind of Engineering Company

EGS — Energex Global Solutions is a professional smart home and electrical engineering company based in North Lebanon. We design, supply, and install complete smart home systems for residential and commercial clients across the region.

We were built on a simple belief: that Lebanese homes deserve smart, reliable, and professionally engineered solutions — not just products dropped in a box. Every project we take on is approached with full engineering discipline, from site assessment to final commissioning.

Our work is grounded in electrical engineering expertise, with a deep understanding of Lebanon's unique infrastructure challenges — power cuts, generator dependency, aging wiring, and unreliable internet. We build systems that work in the real world.

🎯 Mission

To deliver professional, reliable smart home systems that genuinely improve daily life for Lebanese families and businesses.

🔭 Vision

To become the leading smart electrical engineering company in Lebanon, expanding from smart homes to full-scale electrical infrastructure.

⚙️ Engineering First

Every decision is made from an engineering perspective — safety, reliability, and long-term performance come before aesthetics or cost-cutting.

🇱🇧 Lebanon-Focused

We know the market, the infrastructure, and the challenges. Our solutions are built for Lebanese reality, not imported assumptions.
