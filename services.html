Electrical Systems & Power Distribution

Smart electrical infrastructure — built for Lebanon's power realities.

Lebanon's electrical environment is unique — frequent cuts, generator switching, and aging infrastructure. We integrate smart control with your existing electrical system to give you visibility and control over your power consumption and distribution.

1

Generator & Power Monitoring

Install smart circuit breakers and energy meters to monitor consumption in real time — on EDL and generator power separately.

2

Smart Circuit Protection

AI-powered circuit breakers with overload protection, remote on/off, and instant alerts to your phone.

3

Electrical Panel Integration

Connect your distribution board to your smart home system for full visibility and control.
